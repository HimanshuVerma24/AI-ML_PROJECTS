About Our Project

"Old Bike Shell" conjures imagery of resilience and protection, suggesting a project that repurposes old bicycles into vehicles for personal mobility and community connection. The term "shell" evokes notions of shelter and safety, hinting at bicycles as protective cocoons for their riders. This concept symbolizes sustainability and resourcefulness. By breathing new life into discarded bicycles, we embody principles of environmental conservation and waste reduction. Additionally, "shell" implies belonging and interconnectedness with the natural world. Through this initiative, we provide affordable transportation options while fostering a more sustainable and inclusive society.
